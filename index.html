Behes - Debate For All !

For over 10 long years now, Behes has been giving shape to the Indian debate scene by empowering students to become articulate leaders, master the art of persuasion and excel in both academic and real-world settings.

We think, therefore we Are.

Behes isn’t just about debating—it’s a legacy. For over a decade, we’ve been the gateway to a treasure chest of skills, helping thousands of students across India stand out from the crowd. With our vibrant take on the Asian Parliamentary format, Behes has become a nationwide playground for bold ideas, dynamic debates, and intellectual growth, leaving each participant buzzing with confidence and new capabilities.

From empirical evidence to rhetorical finesse, our students have engaged with a vast spectrum of topics that explore the goings on in the world as it keeps spinning, mastering critical thinking, research methodology, and advanced analytical skills. As they sharpen their oratory and collaboration techniques, they gain the precision, clarity, and persuasive authority that sets them apart in any field they choose to pursue.

Host a Regional Behes Round

Passionate about Behes but can’t find a tournament near you? Get in touch with us to explore how you can host an event in your region. As a host, you’ll help us engage schools to participate, while our team will work closely with you to provide all the support and resources needed to ensure a seamless and dynamic tournament experience.

Since Behes is a community-driven initiative, we partner with host schools that share our commitment to making debate accessible to students from diverse backgrounds. Hosting a Behes round goes beyond providing space; it’s about bringing your enthusiasm, expertise, and dedication to create an unforgettable experience for every participant.

Become a Sponsor

As a community-driven initiative, Behes thrives on the support of generous sponsors, making our tournaments not just a reality, but accessible to young minds from all walks of life.

Every sponsor brings something unique to the table. Some help us host key events at regional and national tournaments.

while others provide in-kind donations—whether it’s refreshments or debate materials. Many contribute to our general fund, supporting the continuous evolution of Behes' dynamic learning experience.

We ensure all our sponsors receive due recognition and gratitude throughout every stage of our tournaments, from event promotions to participant outreach. Whether you’re a global organization or a family whose child has grown through Behes, every contribution makes a meaningful impact. For sponsorship opportunities, please reach out to us at contact@behes.org.

Volunteer with Behes

Behes wouldn’t be the vibrant community it is today without the dedicated individuals who volunteer on stage, in the debate rooms, and behind the scenes to bring each tournament to life.

If you're interested in volunteering for an upcoming regional round, send an email to tournament@behes.org and share your experience with Behes, along with the round you'd like to support.

Please note that volunteering is open to those who have completed their journey as Behes participants. We invite students, educators, and parents alike to join us in making our events impactful and memorable!

Ankon Bhattacharya

Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi

"Hey! I am Ankon Bhattacharya, an alumni of Indirapuram Public School, and have been lucky to be trained by Salil sir, in sharpening my debating skills. When I firstly entered the conference room of IPS, I was very confident of my debating skills. But, after the first debate, where I couldn't even continue for 1 minutes, I realised parliamentary debating is something I have to really learn.

From then, there has been no stopping, I had been rigorously trained by Salil Sir my speaking skills have improved a lot. Getting a topic 7 minutes before and prepping a speech for it, can be done with ease due to the training given by them. The training has not only increased my love for debating, which I am still involved in, during my college days but has increased my analytical skills immensely through which I can take a decision based on analysing both sides of the coin and finding out the best ones."
