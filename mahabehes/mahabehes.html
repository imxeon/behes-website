About

The Maha Behes ‘24 is the culmination of this Behes season, where we are proud to invite some of our top budding debaters from over 2000 participants this season. The Maha Behes ‘24 will see people from five countries converge at Sri Venkateshwara International School, Dwarka to battle it out for a shot at the coveted title – a shot at being Maha Behes champions 2024!

The Ball

At the end of the intense debating on Day 1, We will all relax with some amazing performances and ofcourse dancing at our most beloved Mahabehes Ball. Keep your dancing shoes ready, and carry some clothes you can change to! (Or not!) Also, we heard that the DJ is none other than the man whose name is synonymous with excellence in the Behes Community, somebody who has not only been the best debater, but has also proceeded to train the best of adjudicators of the circuit. We surely can't wait! Can you?
