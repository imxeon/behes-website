About

Get ready for the grand finale of the Behes season— Winter Maha Behes '24! We’re thrilled to bring together some of our finest rising stars from over 2,000 participants this year. On December 21st and 22nd, the vibrant Indirapuram Public School will host debaters from all corners of India, all vying for the prestigious title of Maha Behes Champions 2024! Join us as these talented debaters battle it out for glory in an exhilarating showcase of wit and skill!

The Ball

After a thrilling Day 1 of intense debates, it’s time to unwind and celebrate at the much-anticipated Maha Behes Ball! Get ready to dance the night away—don’t forget to pack your dancing shoes and a change of clothes (or not!). This year, like always...... we’re excited to feature a Behes Open Mic, where you can showcase your talents and share your voice with fellow debaters. Plus, there will be a secret DJ session led by someone revered in the Behes community—prepare for an unforgettable night of surprises and electrifying music! We can’t wait for an evening filled with performances, laughter, and celebration and we're sure you can't either.
